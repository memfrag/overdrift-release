Overdrift is a racing game for macOS. It is currently in development and full of glitches. This build should be considered a pre-alpha early access version.

# Important

- The game is meant to be played with a PlayStation controller.
- You can plug in your PlayStation controller with USB to skip pairing with Bluetooth.
- The first time you start the game, it takes a while for it to start, due to various pre-processing.
